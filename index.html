About Us

WE SERVE MORE THAN JUST FOOD

At Saii, we blend passion, flavour and creativity to bring you an unforgettable dining experience. Every dish is crafted with the finest ingredients and a love for perfection.

Discover Our Story

Saii was born from a simple idea - that dining should feel more than just eating. It should feel like a memory.
Inspired by elegance, intimacy, and the beauty of meaningful moments, Saiii blends luxury dining with a warm atmosphere designed to slow time down. even if just for a night.
Behind the name is the inspiration of a young woman whose presence represented grace, comfort, and softness - the same feeling this restaurant was built to give every guest who walks through our doors.
At Saiii, we don't just serve food. we create experiences worth remembering.
"Crafted with passion. Inspired by love"
